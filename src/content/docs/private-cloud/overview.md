# ZSoftly Private Cloud

ZSoftly Private Cloud is a fully managed cloud infrastructure platform deployed on dedicated
hardware, either at your facility or in a ZSoftly-operated data center.

## What it is

A complete, production-grade cloud stack built on open-source infrastructure. After extensive
research and development, ZSoftly fully supports both **Apache CloudStack** and **OpenStack** as the
orchestration layer, and can deliver either one as a **branded self-service portal** for your users
or as direct API and hypervisor access. Either way you get the real platform, not a thin portal
abstraction over someone else's cloud.

## Two deployment models

**On your infrastructure** ZSoftly designs, deploys, and hands over a private cloud on hardware you
own or operate. Useful for regulated industries, data sovereignty requirements, or organizations
with existing data center investments.

**ZSoftly-hosted dedicated** ZSoftly deploys your private cloud in our facilities on dedicated
hardware. You get the isolation and control of a private cloud without managing physical
infrastructure.

In both cases you receive direct access to the orchestration layer (Apache CloudStack or OpenStack),
an optional branded self-service portal with full single sign-on and social login (Keycloak or
Authentik, integrating Google, GitHub, and other OIDC or SAML providers), and ongoing ZSoftly
support.

## Who it's for

- Enterprises with data residency or sovereignty requirements
- Organizations in regulated industries (healthcare, finance, government)
- Managed Service Providers (MSPs) building on a private cloud foundation
- Businesses that need dedicated, predictable infrastructure
